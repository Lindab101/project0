Fashion Industry in Singapore

Problem

The fashion industry is one of the major polluting industries. It is known to be responsible for about 10% of the greenhouse gas emission worldwide (more carbon emissions than the aviation and shipping industries combined), but it is also under fire for polluting the ocean with microplastics. About 60% of clothing materials are made of plastics, including polyester, acrylic and nylon textiles.
